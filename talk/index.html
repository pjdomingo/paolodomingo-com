09 · The Other Side

It's not all good.

Hallucinations
A US lawyer cited ChatGPT-invented court cases in a real filing. The judge found out. The lawyer was sanctioned and publicly shamed.
Deepfakes
A Hong Kong company wired $25M USD to scammers who used AI-generated video to impersonate their CFO on a live call.
Bias
Amazon's internal hiring AI downgraded resumes mentioning "women's" organizations — trained on 10 years of male-dominated hires.
Energy Cost
Training one large model emits ~300 tons of CO₂ — equivalent to 125 round-trip NYC–San Francisco flights. The bigger the model, the bigger the footprint.
