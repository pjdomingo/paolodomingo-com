Integrating modern brutalism with passive organic cooling and sustainable material sourcing.

01 / The Ethos

Passive Harmony.

We do not clear the land to build; we build the structure to allow the land to traverse through it. Eliminating mechanical cooling through aggressive vernacular airflow tunneling.

02 / Materials

Rammed Earth.

Utilizing the exact soil excavated from the site foundation to compress structural walls. Zero transport emissions. Impeccable thermal massing.
