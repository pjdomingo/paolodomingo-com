Process

How I work.

Fast, iterative, and powered by AI. No long timelines. No unnecessary meetings.

01
Day 1
Listen first.

I start by understanding your business, not pitching solutions. One conversation is usually enough to identify where AI can have real impact.

02
Days 2–5
Build fast.

I use AI as a co-pilot — which means what used to take weeks gets done in days. You see working results before the week is out. Agile principles, tight feedback loops.

03
Days 5–7
Ship and improve.

We refine together and launch. After that, I stick around. Small improvements over time, based on how it's actually being used — not a big-bang handover.

The first engagement is often free — I'll build your website and booking system at no cost, so you can see the quality before committing to anything bigger.
